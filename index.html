Motion Coherence Text Decoder

Upload a dot-field video whose message is carried by motion rather than luminance. The tool estimates the global background velocity between consecutive frames to subpixel accuracy, warps and subtracts, and accumulates the residual over short temporal windows. Dots that ride the coherent flow cancel; static or incoherent dots do not, and the text appears. It then binarizes the sharpest window and runs OCR to return the message as a string. Frame extraction and decoding are fully local; only the optional OCR step fetches a library from a CDN.
